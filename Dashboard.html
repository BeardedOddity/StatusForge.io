Twitch (OAuth2)
?
How to get your Twitch Client ID & Secret:

1. Go to the Twitch Developer Console and log in.
2. Click Applications -> Register Your Application.
3. Fill out the form exactly like this:
   • Name: StatusForge Local (or anything you want)
   • OAuth Redirect URLs: http://localhost:5050/twitch/callback
      (⚠️ CRITICAL: Must be exact!)
   • Category: Application Integration
4. Click Create.
5. Click Manage on your new app.
6. Copy your Client ID. Then, click New Secret and copy your Client Secret. Paste both below.

Kick (OAuth2 PKCE)
?
How to get your Kick Client ID & Secret:

1. Go to your Kick Creator Dashboard -> Developer / API.
2. Click Create Application.
3. Fill out the form exactly like this:
   • App Name: StatusForge Local
   • Redirect URI: http://localhost:5050/kick/callback
      (⚠️ CRITICAL: Must be exact!)
4. Click Create.
5. Copy the Client ID & Client Secret below.
